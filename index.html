Football

Football, also known as soccer in some parts of the world, is one of the most popular and widely played sports globally. It is a team sport played between two teams, each consisting of 11 players, on a rectangular field.
Here are some key aspects and interesting facts about football: Origins: The modern form of football originated in England in the mid-19th century.,
However, variations of the game have been played for centuries in different cultures around the world. Objective: The objective of the game is to score goals by maneuvering the ball into the opponent's net, while defending one's own net to prevent the opposing team from scoring.
Field and Equipment: A football field is typically 100-110 meters long and 64-75 meters wide. The players wear jerseys, shorts, socks, and cleats (football boots), and each team has a goalkeeper who wears a different color jersey.
